Welcome to the Transition Resources for Youth website, or simply, tr4y.

This website offers youth, families, educators and agencies high quality transition tools and resources designed to improve the transition planning process and ultimately enhance the lives and post school outcomes of youth with disabilities. Register for and account to use the tools and save favorite resources, or simply search our high quality resources. New resources are added daily, so check back often to see what's new!

Using TR4Y will assist users in enhancing the transition planning process for improved post school outcomes of youth with disabilities. As you get started using the website, determine how you will use the website and if you need to register for a TR4Y account.
